After-Hours Care

Our hours are Mon-Fri 8:30-5:00 and we are closed on weekends.
If you need medical assistance outside of these hours please look at the list below to find an appropriate option.

Emergency Medical Attention

If you or someone you are with is experiencing a medical emergency please dial 9-1-1

Non-Emergency Medical Attention Call Line

If you need to speak with a medical professional, please contact the British Columbia provincial health information and advice phone line by dialing 8-1-1. This service is free of charge and avaiable 24/7. You can be connected with a with a registered nurse, a registered dietitian, a qualified exercise professional, or a pharmacist.

Walk-In Clinics in Vernon B.C.

If we are closed you may also check Medimaps to see if there is a walk in Clinic open. Please click here for Medimaps Vernon, if you are not in this area you can adjust the location.

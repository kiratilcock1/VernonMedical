Main Street Medical West is located at 3207 30th Ave, Vernon, British Columbia on the 2nd floor. All of your medical needs can be covered conveniently in one place.

Shared Practice

Your health needs can't wait. Our working group ensures that there is always a doctor to see you, even if your doctor is unavailable.

Lab

Valley Medical Laboratories located in our building produces results quickly and reliably.

Pharmacy

No need to go across town to get a prescription filled. Nolan's Clinic Pharmacy has got you covered, conveniently located on the same level as our offices.

Travel Medical Clinic

Planning a trip? Visit the Travel Medicine and Vaccination Centre. Located in the basement of our building.

Councillors

Your mental health is just as important as your physical health. Freedom Counselling, found on the same floor as our offices can help you get back on your feet.

Doctors with Hospital Privileges

At the hospital it's always nice to see a familiar face. Many of our doctors have hospital privileges, so you can see your family doctor at the hospital too.
